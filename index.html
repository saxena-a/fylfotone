Fylfotone Ventures Private Limited — Building a Better Future Through Deep Tech

Fylfotone (also known as Fylfot One or Fylfot) is a multigenerational venture ecosystem founded by Aryan Saxena in India, 2025. The company builds at the intersection of science, technology, society, economics, and human behavior, working to restore societal trust through first-principles thinking and innovation.

Aryan Saxena is a deep-tech researcher, builder, and strategic leader working on privacy-first decentralized systems. He is an alumnus and ambassador of the UZH Blockchain Center at the University of Zurich, a Founding Beyonder at Atria University pursuing BTech in Digital Transformation, and has published research on decentralized applications, zero-knowledge proofs, cryptography, multi-party computation, and blockchain cybersecurity. He has worked with Sacura and Oasis Protocol on privacy-preserving blockchain infrastructure.
